# 🏔️ 五界体系 / The Five Worlds System

## I. 【Physical World | 物质界】

*The lowest world, solid reality.*

*最底层的世界，坚实、可触的真实。*

*All visible and tangible beings manifest here.*

*一切可见可触的存在，都在此处显化为真实。*

*This is the end point of "as above, so below".*

*这里是"如在上，如在下"的终点站。*

*Your body, this planet, all things emerge from here.*

*你的身体、这颗星球、世间的万物，都从这里诞生。*

## II. ⚡【Etheric World | 以太界】

*The realm of vital energy flow.*

*生命力流动的领域。*

*It serves as the blueprint of the physical world.*

*它是物质世界的蓝图，一切模板的源头。*

*Prana, chi, chakras—all belong here.*

*气、脉轮——都属于这里。*

*Connecting the movements of seasons and life's vitality.*

*它连接着四季轮转与万物的生命力。*

## III. 🌈【Astral World | 星光界】

*The plane of emotion and desire.*

*情感与欲望交织的层面。*

*Your love, fear, longing, dreams—all weave here.*

*你的爱、恐惧、渴望、梦境——都在此处流转编织。*

*It is lighter than the etheric, full of color and form.*

*它比以太界更轻盈，充满了色彩与形态。*

*The first realm the soul reaches after death.*

*人死后，灵魂首先到达的地方就是这里。*

## IV. 💭【Mental World | 心智界】

*The domain of pure thought and wisdom.*

*纯粹思想与智慧交汇的领域。*

*Concepts, ideas, and creativity take full shape here.*

*概念、理念、创造力，都在这里成形。*

*The consciousness of "I AM" expands here.*

*"我存在"的意识，在这里延展扩大。*

*The bridge connecting the individual self to the higher soul.*

*它连接着个体的自我与更高的灵魂。*

## V. ✨【Spirit World | 灵魂界】

*The source of cosmic consciousness, the unity of all.*

*宇宙意识的源头，万物合一的所在。*

*Beyond individuality, returning to the source where "all is one".*

*超越个体性，回归到"一切皆是一体"的本源。*

*Pure being, eternal wisdom.*

*纯粹的存在，超越时间的智慧。*

*As above, so below—here is that "above".*

*如在上，如在下——这里，就是那个"上"。*

## 🔗 五界关系 / Interconnections

*These five worlds are not separate prisons, but interlocking ladders.*

*这五界并非彼此隔绝的牢笼，而是相互渗透的阶梯。*

*As you ascend, you remain within them.*

*当你向上攀升时，你仍在它们之中；*

*When you return, you bring your whole self.*

*当你回归时，你带着完整的自己。*
