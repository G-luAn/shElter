# 物质界 / Physical World

## 概述

*The foundation of all existence, the tangible reality we inhabit.*

*一切存在的基础，我们栖居的可触现实。*

*This is the world of form, matter, and physical manifestation.*

*这是形态、物质和物理显化的世界。*

## 核心特征

*Solid, measurable, and observable reality.*

*坚实、可测量、可观察的现实。*

*The endpoint of "as above, so below"—where ideas become form.*

*"如在上，如在下"的终点站——理念在此化为形态。*

*Everything you can touch, see, and experience with your physical senses.*

*一切你能触摸、看见、用物理感官体验的事物。*
